Rules

1. You need to guess a number between 1 and 100.
2. If your guess is less than 1 or greater than 100, it will be considered "OUT OF BOUNDS."
3. On your first turn:
a. If your guess is within 10 of the number, you'll be told "WARM!"
b. If your guess is further than 10 away from the number, you'll be told "COLD!"
4. On subsequent turns:
a. If your guess is closer to the number than your previous guess, you'll be told "WARMER!"
b. If your guess is farther from the number than your previous guess, you'll be told "COLDER!"
5. If your guess equals the number, you'll be informed that you've guessed correctly and how many guesses it took!
6. You have 20 guesses.
7. HighScore will be displayed.
